Cambridge Auto Center

Kristy Allen

Kristy is the owner of Cambridge Auto Center, and has been a part of The Christian School at Castle Hills family since 2014 when her daughter started attending. Started by her father, Cambridge Auto Center opened its doors in 1984 and has been offering great automotive service and repair ever since by providing dealer quality service with free shuttle, a comfortable waiting area, complimentary carwash and vacuum, and a 2 year/24,000 mile warranty! They service all types of vehicles both foreign and domestic. Cambridge Auto Center is also a AAA approved repair facility, employing only Master certified ASE technicians.

Charles Ward

Charles and his family arrived at The Christian School at Castle Hills in 2014 and immediately fell in love with it. Charles has been giving back to the school by volunteer coaching the 5th and 6th grade girls basketball teams.

Charles served in the United States Air Force and spent 16 years in the corporate world. He now enjoys a career that allows him to help people by assisting small businesses, associations and other qualifying groups with making supplemental benefits a reality in their environments, via Voluntary Benefits plans as an Independent Agent. This allows him to provide access to supplemental products to individuals and families through a direct, one to one, format.
