Cambridge Auto Center

Kristy Allen

Kristy is the owner of Cambridge Auto Center. Started by her father, it opened its doors in 1984 and has been offering great automotive service and repair ever since. Providing dealer quality service with free shuttle service, a comfortable waiting area, complimentary carwash and vacuum, and a 2 year/24,000 mile warranty (better than the dealer)! They service all types of vehicles both foreign and domestic.
