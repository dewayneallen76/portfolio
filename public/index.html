Mission Statement: When we grow, Castle Hills grows

The mission of Eagle Parents Business Network is to provide a setting for parents that are small business owners at The Christian School at Castle Hills that want their small business to grow, improve, provide and receive referrals for participating businesses operating near and beyond the Castle Hills area.

Our focus of providing participants the foundation to promote goodwill and fellowship in concert with the necessary spiritual, emotional and professional support each participant brings to the group.

Within the group, individual business growth is our prime objective, with earnest effort to Service(God, Family, Friends, School and those we're fortunate to call Customers) and give back to the school community.

What Can It Do For You? What Can You Do For It?

Our members consist of parents and grandparents who are also business owners and business professionals whether it's brick and mortar, representative, home-based, direct sale, or anything in between!

The purpose of our group is to enrich and establish business and personal relationships withing the community of The Christian School at Castle Hills. Our ulitmate goal is to help strengthen, promote and serve the school by growing and promoting one another's businessess. We feel that when we grow in business we help grow The Christian School at Castle Hills.
